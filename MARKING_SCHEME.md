# Marking Scheme (Team Component)

**Total: 100 marks**

---

## 1. Basic Functional Requirements (40 marks)

| Task | Description | Marks |
|------|-------------|-------|
| a) | Add a new area | 3 |
| b) | Add a new resident | 4 |
| c) | Associate the new resident with the new area | 5 |
| d) | Add a new product for an existing CulturalConnect platform | 4 |
| e) | Add a new vote by the new resident for the new product or service | 8 |
| f) | Edit data by changing the existing CulturalConnect platform for the products or services | 4 |
| g) | Drill downs: search/filter the products. Display all products and services which are classified based on the CulturalConnect product/service | 4 |
| h) | Drill downs: search/filter the products or services. Display all those products or services which are classified based on the CulturalConnect product/service and for which the price is less than £200 | 8 |

**Criterion:** Mostly clear processing steps and data examples. Lower marks for incomplete/less detail.

---

## 2. Advanced Functional Requirements (10 marks)

**Choose ONE only (not all):**

| Option | Description | Marks |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Data Reporting | Display a view (via custom SQL) showing the results of all products that residents are interested in, ranked from highest to lowest score. Ensure the product name and price of each CulturalConnect platform product or service are listed beside their respective total scores. | 10 |
| System Access Rights | Entry to the interface via a login screen with restriction of pages in the navigation and/or functionality (such as read/write) for different users. The system shouldn't be accessed without a login. | 10 |
| Batch Processing | The ability to set availability for all products provided by a specific CulturalConnect platform. Include a custom form field box(es) with input criteria to select a platform and alter their products to 'available' or 'unavailable'. Unavailable products should not be possible to vote for (by restricting data). | 10 |
| Other | Other additional feature(s) chosen by you. | 10 |

**Criterion:** Mostly clear processing steps and data examples. Lower marks for incomplete/less detailed.
